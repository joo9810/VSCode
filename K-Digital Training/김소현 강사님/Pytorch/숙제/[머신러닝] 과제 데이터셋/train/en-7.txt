Culex (Lophoceraomyia) mammilifer is a species of mosquito belonging to the genus Culex. It is found in Cambodia, China, India, the Andaman Islands, Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Sri Lanka,[1] and Thailand.[2][3]
